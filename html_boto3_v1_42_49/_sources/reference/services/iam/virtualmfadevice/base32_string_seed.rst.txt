base32_string_seed
******************



.. py:attribute:: IAM.VirtualMfaDevice.base32_string_seed

  

  - *(bytes) --* 

    The base32 seed defined as specified in `RFC3548 <https://tools.ietf.org/html/rfc3548.txt>`__. The ``Base32StringSeed`` is base32-encoded.
