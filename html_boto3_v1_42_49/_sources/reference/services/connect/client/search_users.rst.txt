************
search_users
************



.. py:method:: Connect.Client.search_users(**kwargs)

  

  Searches users in an Amazon Connect instance, with optional filtering.

   

  .. note::

    

    ``AfterContactWorkTimeLimit`` is returned in milliseconds.
