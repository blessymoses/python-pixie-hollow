======
Client
======



.. py:class:: LocationService.Client

  A low-level client representing Amazon Location Service
  

  "Suite of geospatial services including Maps, Places, Routes, Tracking, and Geofencing"

  ::

    
    import boto3
    
    client = boto3.client('location')
