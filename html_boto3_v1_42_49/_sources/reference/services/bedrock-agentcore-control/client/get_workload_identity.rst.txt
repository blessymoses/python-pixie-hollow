.. py:method:: BedrockAgentCoreControl.Client.get_workload_identity(**kwargs)

  **Request Syntax**

    response = client.get_workload_identity(
        name='string'
    )

  :param name: **[REQUIRED]**

  :rtype: dict
  :returns:
