  :type RackPhysicalProperties: dict
  :param RackPhysicalProperties: 

    Information about the physical and logistical details for the rack at this site.     - **UplinkCount** *(string) --* 

      The number of uplinks each Outpost network device.

      

    
    - **FiberOpticCableType** *(string) --* 

      The type of fiber used to attach the Outpost to the network.

      

    
    - **OpticalStandard** *(string) --* 

      The type of optical standard used to attach the Outpost to the network. This field is dependent on uplink speed, fiber type, and distance to the upstream device.     - **MaximumSupportedWeightLbs** *(string) --* 

      The maximum rack weight that this site can support. ``NO_LIMIT`` is over 2000 lbs (907 kg).
