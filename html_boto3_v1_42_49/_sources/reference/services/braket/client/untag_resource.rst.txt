.. py:method:: Braket.Client.untag_resource(**kwargs)

        tagKeys=[

        ]

  :param resourceArn: **[REQUIRED]** 

    Specify the ``resourceArn`` for the resource from which to remove the tags.

    

  
  :type tagKeys: list
