availability_zone
*****************



.. py:attribute:: EC2.Snapshot.availability_zone

  

  - *(string) --* 

    The Availability Zone or Local Zone of the snapshot. For example, ``us-west-1a`` (Availability Zone) or ``us-west-2-lax-1a`` (Local Zone).
